Privacy Policy - BreakUp?

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

BreakUp? is a minimal rational breakup decision assistant designed to help users make informed relationship decisions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Developer: ffge
Contact: dounengce@163.com

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address above.

2. Privacy-First Design

BreakUp? is built with privacy as a core principle:

  • No Account Required: You can use all features without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Local-Only Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behavior, use analytics, or collect any identifiers.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information Stored Locally Only

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage:

Data Type Description Purpose
Relationship Test Answers Your responses to the 20-question relationship health assessment To generate your relationship health score and AI recommendations
Generated Scripts Breakup conversation scripts you generate To allow you to view, copy, or modify your scripts
Diary Entries Your mood selections and written diary entries To help you track your emotional journey after a breakup
App Preferences Language settings and app configuration To provide a personalized experience

This data never leaves your device unless explicitly stated below.

3.2 Information Processed by AI Service

When you use the relationship health test or script generator features:

  • Your test answers or selected scenario are temporarily transmitted to an AI service (DeepSeek API) for processing.
  • The AI service generates recommendations or scripts based on your input.
  • We do not store your inputs or the AI responses on any servers.
  • The AI service provider's privacy policy applies to the processing of your data during transmission.

We do not share any personally identifiable information with the AI service.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

BreakUp? does NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Social media account information
  • Photos or videos
  • Voice or audio data

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • You control your data. Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • No cloud backup. We do not back up your data to any cloud service.
  • Data deletion. You can delete your data at any time within the app, or by uninstalling the app.
  • No data recovery. If you uninstall the app, all your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Service Provider

We use the following third-party service:

  • DeepSeek API: For generating relationship health recommendations and breakup scripts.

Please review the DeepSeek Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data during AI processing.

No other third-party services are used. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integration
  • Crash reporting services

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption where available.
  • Data transmitted to the AI service is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • We do not have access to your device's local storage.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

BreakUp? is intended for users who are at least 17 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at dounengce@163.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the app after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data within the app or by uninstalling the app.
  • Modification: You can edit your diary entries, scripts, and test results within the app.
  • Data Portability: Since all data is stored locally, you retain full control and access.

11. International Data Transfers

When you use AI features, your data may be transmitted to servers located outside your country. The AI service provider is responsible for handling such transfers in accordance with their privacy policy.
